# Phylum Arthropoda
 Triploblastic, bilaterally symmetrical & metamerically segmented animals. Body segments are groped into 2 regions- cephalothorax and abdomen, or 3 regions- head, thorax & abdomen. Anterior part of body forms a distinct head, bearing sense organs and brain. Example- Palaemon , Cyclops , Palamnaeus , Aranea .
